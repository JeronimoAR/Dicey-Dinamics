Spellcrafters

Overview

Welcome young mage. gather your magic, master the elements and craft your spells to over power your opponents become the are you ready to become the greatest sorcerer of them all.



SpellCrafters is a tableau building game where players battle it out to become the greatest sorcerer of them all, play spell cards, increase your mastery of the elements in your spellbooks, and gain points to be the victor.

Requirements

  • Age 8+
  • 2-4 players
  • play time: 45 - 60 min
