Knives Out: A Murder Mystery

Overview

You have been invited to tech billionaire Miles Bron's Glass Onion island for a 3-day weekend getaway to solve a murder mystery alongside your mentor, master detective, Benoit Blanc. What started off as a game has suddenly become all too real after one of the guests is murdered. You and Detective Blanc must work together with the remaining guests to find out who the killer is! Beware, because the killer also has an accomplice. Use your detective skills to solve the mystery before the killer murders the majority of the guests, or else he or she will murder you too! A mix of the famous games Clue and Mafia, this game is a party game like no other that will test your cunning and intuition.

Requirements

  • Age: 8+
  • 8 or more players
  • Play Time: 30 - 90 minutes
